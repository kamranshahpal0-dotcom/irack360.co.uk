— About Us

A Family-Run UK Racking Specialist

Based in Blackburn, Lancashire, iRack360 is an independent, family-run business specialising in all aspects of industrial storage.

With many years of experience in the warehousing and storage industry, we understand that every business has different needs. That's why we offer a personal service — we listen, advise, and provide exactly what works for you.

We operate throughout the whole of the UK, working with small businesses, large companies, manufacturers, distributors and retailers.

Safety is at the heart of everything we do. All our work meets current UK regulations and SEMA guidelines, and we are fully insured and certified.

Our goal is simple: high quality work, fair prices, and customers who come back to us again and again.
